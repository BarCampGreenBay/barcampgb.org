BarCamp Green Bay is a participant-led day of talks, discussions, and activities for sharing knowledge. There's no cost to attend.

Participant-Led

Everyone is invited to propose a session topic, and everyone helps make the day run smoothly. This is your chance to share that thing you love.

Diverse

Any topic is welcome – it's all about sharing knowledge. Past topics include how to crochet a cube, retro gaming, emotional intelligence, and web development. Have a look at last year's proposals.

Safe

BarCamp Green Bay is dedicated to a harassment-free conference experience for everyone. Please read our code of conduct.

Family-Friendly

Every year we organize a KidsCamp with fun things for children to do so bring yours along and encourage them to present too!
