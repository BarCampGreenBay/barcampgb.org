Barcamp is diverse

It’s a day of talks, discussions, and activities on an open-ended variety of topics. Past topics include artificial intelligence, retro gaming, emotional intelligence, and web development. Anything is welcome – it’s all about sharing knowledge.

Barcamp is participant-led

Everyone is invited to propose a session topic, and everyone helps to make sure the day goes smoothly. This is your chance to share that thing you love.

Barcamp is free

There’s no cost to attend Barcamp. It’s an open environment for sharing knowledge.

How it works

  1. Register to attend – it’s free!
  2. Submit a session topic – a talk, demo, discussion, workshop… whatever works for you.
  3. Help out – spread the word, help with setup during the event, contact us to see where you can help.
  4. Join us November 1st – we’ll plan the day’s schedule as a group and get going!
