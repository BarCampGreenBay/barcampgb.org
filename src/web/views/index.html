BarCamp Green Bay is a participant-led day of talks, discussions, and activities for sharing knowledge. There's no cost to attend.

Participant-led

Everyone is invited to propose a session topic, and everyone helps to make sure the day goes smoothly. This is your chance to share that thing you love.

Diverse

Any topic is welcome for a BarCamp session – it's all about sharing knowledge. Past topics include artificial intelligence, retro gaming, emotional intelligence, and web development.

Safe

BarCamp Green Bay is dedicated to a harassment-free conference experience for everyone. Please read our code of conduct.
